Note Taking Templates

Cornell Method

Cue Column: Keywords or questions go here.

Main Notes Column: Take notes here.

Summary: Summarize the information in this section.

Blurting Method

Write down everything that comes to your mind related to the topic. Don't worry about order or structure.

Outlining Method

Main Topic:

  • Subtopic 1
  • Subtopic 2
  • Subtopic 3

Mind Mapping Method

Create a central idea and branch out with related concepts.

Charting Method

Create a table with columns and rows to organize information.

Sentence Method

Write complete sentences to summarize key points.

Quadrant Method

Divide the page into four quadrants to organize information.
